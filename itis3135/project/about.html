About Skyrise

What is Skyise?

Skyrise is a club ultimate frisbee team at the college of UNC Charlotte. The program first started back in 2014. We are a D1 team and very welcoming to players of all skill levels. Whether you are a seasoned veteran that has played for many years or you have never touched the disc before we welcome people from all backgrounds of the sport.

History of Skyrise

Skyrise started off as a ragtag team that was known to not take the sport seriously and be toxic. In 2019 Skyrise did not qualify for regionals but the following year Skyrise managed to just barely barely qualify for regionals. In the 2023 season a new team emerged. Skyrise was a reformed team team that cared about the sport. That year the team made a very good run at nationals. This year the team is on pace to go to nationals.
